Life at The Anchor
Our Story
A Marina Institution

Nestled in the heart of Swansea's beautiful Maritime Quarter, The Anchor has been welcoming guests to its warm and vibrant atmosphere for years. Whether you're here for a relaxed lunch by the water or a special evening dinner, we pride ourselves on delivering outstanding food and service every time.

Our kitchen team craft everything with passion — from our stone-baked pizzas and handmade pasta, to our fresh seafood and premium grills. There's something for everyone on our menus.

Freshly prepared food, every day
Stunning marina waterfront views
Fully licensed bar with extensive wine list
